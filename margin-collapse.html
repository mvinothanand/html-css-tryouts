Margin Collapsing: In the vertical direction, the smaller of the adjacent margins merge with the larger.

Line 1:This is a block <p> element with margin: 30px auto

Line 2:This is a block <p> element with margin: 10px auto; set.

The bottom and top margins of line 1 and line 2 collapses and the larger bottom margin of line 1, 30px remains between them.

Verify this using the developer tools.

Note: margin top and bottom are not applicable for inline elements. Hence, the previous/next block or inline-block element's margin top/bottom gets applied.
